The approach

A practical herbal reference you'll actually use.

Most herbal books are inspiring but difficult to navigate when you need something specific. The Modern Home Apothecary was created as a working reference — not a coffee-table book.

Every recipe and herb profile is organized for clarity, speed, and real-world use.

Structure

Designed to be used, not guessed through.

Inside the book you'll find:

  • A clear index by purpose (daily routines, sleep, digestion, skin care, seasonal comfort)
  • Preparation-based sections (teas, tinctures, salves, syrups, oils, baths)
  • Time & complexity tags (quick, overnight, beginner-friendly)
  • Alcohol-free alternatives where applicable
  • Safety notes and general use considerations throughout

Everything is structured so you can go from question to preparation quickly and confidently.
