FAQs

What are the time and costs involved to develop and execute a new concept?

Each concept development is unique, with different sets of costs and timelines, depending on your needs. We take time to listen to your brief before creating a detailed proposal once we have a better understanding of your project.

Drop us an e-mail to tell us about your project large or small. 

Can I pick and choose different elements i.e. could we focus on one aspect for example menu development or training? 

Absolutely, we offer a range of services suited to your requirements, get in touch to learn more. 

Can you support with operational requirements, equipment, set-up and technical drawings? 

Yes we pride ourselves on the global network of suppliers we have built up over the years and we truly understand the potential challenges that you may face so we can support you through this process. This includes designing or optimising existing bars and service areas to ensure optimal functionality and the smoothest most profitable operations.

Do you help with recruitment?

We do, we have strong global network of service and hospitality professionals and we can help you at every stage of the recruitment process and then ensure you retain your talent. 

Can you help with design and collateral?

We have team and network of designers with years of experience developing and designing market leading collateral and designs for some of the best brands in the world.

Do you support with social media strategies

Absolutely, it’s so important to connect the dots between the offering and your PR, marketing and social media strategies, we will work with your team to ensure you are projecting the right messages and imagery out into the world to attract and connect with your future clients and guests. 

Do you create immersive experiences for events?

Yes we do, we work with luxury brands, wedding planners, educational centres, artists... anyone who would like to create some beautiful elevated rituals and stories for their guests. Do get in touch to learn more. 

Still have questions?

If you have any questions at all, would like to collaborate or would just like to reach out, please do not hesitate.
