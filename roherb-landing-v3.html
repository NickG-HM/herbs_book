Origin

The Story Behind the RoHerb Book

This book started from a simple idea: the best botanical drinks should not depend on a bar or a pro setup. We wanted to give you the same clarity and care at home. Clear recipes, no guesswork, so you can build a real practice in your own kitchen.

Every recipe has been tested for home use. Whether you are new to herbs or already making your own infusions, the book is designed to be your go-to: organised, easy to follow, and focused entirely on what you can do at home.
